How it works

  1. Install the app.
  2. Get assigned an anonymous ID.
  3. CovidApp continuously scans your immediate surroundings for devices within 2m with CovidApp installed. This is considered the COVID-19 exposure zone.
  4. The app periodically sends a list of anonymous IDs from those devices to a secure cloud server.
  5. If somebody that you've encountered tests positive for COVID-19, you'll receive a warning based on advice from your local health service.

Working with governments & health partners

We are working closely with governments, health services and research centers to provide a rapid, automated contact tracing solution for COVID-19, enabling them make more informed public health decisions.

Our partners will be helping to verify COVID-19 cases, keep our system up-to-date with best practices and distribute the service worldwide.

100% anonymous

As a non-profit, we're not in the business of mining your data. Any information we collect is strictly necessary for the functioning of the service and informing public health decisions.

To ensure we uphold the highest standard of privacy, we will be releasing all code as open source and working with our partners to ensure the system is 100% anonymous.

This means:

  • We don't track GPS locations, so we don't know where you've been.

  • We don't require a name, email address or mobile number.

  • We don't show your information on a public map.

Help us to save lives

The coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic is one of the biggest crises humankind has seen in modern times. It has a case fatality rate of nearly 15% in people over 80 years of age, and people with underlying conditions are also highly susceptible. If we don't take action, millions of people will die.

Young people are less susceptible to fatality, but can help to spread the disease. From what we currently know, an infected person will infect 2 additional people on average.

Until we have effective treatments, it is important to limit the spread of the virus to protect the most vulnerable. One of the most effective methods for limiting the spread of COVID-19 is to trace all contacts of someone that has been confirmed to have been infected. This is known as contact tracing.

Contact tracing involves identifying everyone that an infected person has been in contact with and providing them with health advice, such as instructions to quarantine themselves and prevent the spread.

As the number of cases has grown, this process has become unmanageable, resulting in governments around the world to locking down entire countries to prevent the spread.

CovidApp enables governments and public health services to rapidly trace all contacts for an infected person, issuing targeted advice directly to them. This means they're able to use new approaches to tackling coronavirus that don't necessarily involve locking a country down.

The more people using the network, the more effective it can be in tackling coronavirus. Please, download the app and help us to save lives.
